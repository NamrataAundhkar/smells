Duplicate Abstraction

This smell arises when two or more abstractions have identical names or identical implementation or both.

It is also known as "Alternative classes with different interfaces" [Fowler] and "Duplicate design artifacts" [Stal].

Reference

Girish Suryanarayana, Ganesh Samarthyam, and Tushar Sharma, Refactoring for Software Design Smells: Managing Technical Debt, 1st ed. Morgan Kaufmann, 2014.
