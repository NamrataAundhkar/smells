Multifaceted Abstraction

This smell arises when an abstraction has more than one responsibility assigned to it.

The smell is also known as "Divergent change" [Fowler], "Conceptualization abuse" [Trifu], and "Lack of cohesion" [Semmle].

Reference

Girish Suryanarayana, Ganesh Samarthyam, and Tushar Sharma, Refactoring for Software Design Smells: Managing Technical Debt, 1st ed. Morgan Kaufmann, 2014.
