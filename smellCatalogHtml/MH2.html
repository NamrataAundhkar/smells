Multipath Hierarchy

This smell arises when a subtype inherits both directly as well as indirectly from a supertype leading to unnecessary inheritance paths in the hierarchy.

It is also referred as "Degenerate inheritance" [Sonargraph,Beyer] and "Repeated inheritance" [MeyerB].

Reference

Girish Suryanarayana, Ganesh Samarthyam, and Tushar Sharma, Refactoring for Software Design Smells: Managing Technical Debt, 1st ed. Morgan Kaufmann, 2014.
