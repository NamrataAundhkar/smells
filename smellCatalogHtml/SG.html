Speculative Generality

This smell occurs where an abstraction is created based on speculated requirements. It is often unnecessary that makes things difficult to understand and maintain.

Related smells: Unutilized Abstraction Speculative Hierarchy

Reference

Fowler M. Refactoring: improving the design of existing code. Boston, MA, USA: Addison-Wesley Longman Publishing Co., Inc.; 1999.
