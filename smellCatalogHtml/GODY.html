The Grand Old Duke of York

This smell occurs when developers (or implementationists) couldn't identify the significance of good abstractions and ignore them even after suggested by some team members (referred as abstractionist).

Related smells: Missing Abstraction

Reference

W. Brown, R. Malveau, H. McCormick, and T. Mowbray, AntiPatterns: Refactoring software, architectures, and projects in crisis. John Wiley & Sons, 1998.
