Swiss Army Knife

This smell arises when the designer attempts to provide all possible uses of the class and ends up in an excessively complex class interface.

Related smells: Multifaceted Abstraction Insufficient Modularization

Reference

W. Brown, R. Malveau, H. McCormick, and T. Mowbray, AntiPatterns: Refactoring software, architectures, and projects in crisis. John Wiley & Sons, 1998.
