Broken Hierarchy

The use of inheritance must be limited to the same module. The smell occurs when, the inheritance is used across namespaces where inheritance is not natural ('is-a' relationship is not followed).

Reference

Tushar Sharma, Marios Fragkoulis, and Diomidis Spinellis, “Does your configuration code smell?” in Proceedings of the 13th International Workshop on Mining Software Repositories, ser. MSR’16, 2016, pp. 189–200.
