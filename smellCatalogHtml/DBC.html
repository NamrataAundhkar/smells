Design by Committee

This smell arises when a dedicated committee is formed to design and architecture the system. Design by Committee creates overly complex architectures that lack coherence.

Related smells:

Reference

W. Brown, R. Malveau, H. McCormick, and T. Mowbray, AntiPatterns: Refactoring software, architectures, and projects in crisis. John Wiley & Sons, 1998.
