Golden Hammer

This smell results in the misapplication of a favored tool, library, language, framework, or concept. Developers and managers are comfortable with an existing approach and unwilling to learn and apply one that is better suited.

Related smells:

Reference

W. Brown, R. Malveau, H. McCormick, and T. Mowbray, AntiPatterns: Refactoring software, architectures, and projects in crisis. John Wiley & Sons, 1998.
