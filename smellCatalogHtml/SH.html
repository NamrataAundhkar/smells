Speculative Hierarchy

This smell arises when one or more types in a hierarchy are provided speculatively (i.e., based on imagined needs rather than real requirements).

Related smells: Speculative Generality

Reference

Girish Suryanarayana, Ganesh Samarthyam, and Tushar Sharma, Refactoring for Software Design Smells: Managing Technical Debt, 1st ed. Morgan Kaufmann, 2014.
