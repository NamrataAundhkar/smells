Hub-like Modularization

This smell arises when an abstraction has dependencies (both incoming and outgoing) with a large number of other abstractions.

This smell is also known as "Bottlenecks" [Sonargraph, Page-Jones], "Local hubs" [Stan4J] and "Man-in-the-middle" [Ratzinger].

Reference

Girish Suryanarayana, Ganesh Samarthyam, and Tushar Sharma, Refactoring for Software Design Smells: Managing Technical Debt, 1st ed. Morgan Kaufmann, 2014.
