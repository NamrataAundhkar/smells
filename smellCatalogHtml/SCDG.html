Static Cycles in Dependency Graphs

Two classes using each other constitute the simplest imaginable cycle in a dependency graph. The presence of many cycles in a system will lead to its lumping.

Related smells: Cyclically-dependent Modularization Dependency Cycles between Packages/Subsystems

Reference

Lippert M, Roock S. Refactoring in large software projects: performing complex restructurings successfully. John Wiley and Sons; 2006.
